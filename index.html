About

I am an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at Southern Utah University. My work focuses on theoretical computer science, especially computational geometry and related algorithmic problems.

I am completing my Ph.D. in Computer Science at the Kahlert School of Computing, University of Utah, where I conduct research under the supervision of Dr. Haitao Wang. I began my Ph.D. studies at Utah State University in Fall 2021 and transferred to the University of Utah in Fall 2022.

Prior to my doctoral studies, I received my M.S. in Computer Science from the University of Utah in 2020 and my B.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Utah in 2017.

Research Interests

My research is centered on geometric and combinatorial problems in theoretical computer science.
