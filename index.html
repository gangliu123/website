About

I am an Assistant Professor of Computer Science in the Department of Computer Science & Cybersecurity at Southern Utah University.

My research focuses on theoretical computer science, especially computational geometry and related algorithmic problems. In particular, I study geometric coverage problems, hitting set problems, and geometric optimization.

I completed my Ph.D. in Computer Science at the Kahlert School of Computing, University of Utah, advised by Dr. Haitao Wang. Before that, I received my M.S. in Computing and B.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Utah.

Research Interests

My research is centered on geometric and combinatorial problems in theoretical computer science.
